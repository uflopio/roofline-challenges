Perform 3D tensor-matrix multiplication of two tensors:
$$
C_{i,j}[l] = \sum_{k=0}^{K-1} A_{i,j}[k] \cdot B_{k,l}
$$

## Input
- Tensor $A$ of size $N \times M \times K$
- Matrix $B$ of size $K \times L$

## Output
- Tensor $C$ of size $N \times M \times L$

## Sizes

- $N = 32, M = 2^{12}, K = 2^{12}, L = 2^{12}$
- $N = 16, M = 2^{13}, K = 2^{13}, L = 2^{12}$
- $N = 64, M = 2^{12}, K = 2^{12}, L = 2^{13}$
- $N = 8, M = 2^{13}, K = 2^{13}, L = 2^{13}$
