Compute the row-wise sum of a matrix.

Given matrix $A$ of shape $M \times N$, produce vector $\underline{r}$ of length $M$ such that

$$
r_i = \sum_{j=0}^{N-1} A_{i,j} \quad \text{for } i = 0, 1, \dots, M - 1.
$$

## Input

- One matrix $A$ of shape $M \times N$ in row-major order.

## Output

- One vector $\underline{r}$ of length $M$ such that $r_i = \sum_j A_{i,j}$.

## Sizes

- $M = 2^{10}$, $N = 2^{10}$
- $M = 2^{12}$, $N = 2^{12}$
- $M = 2^{14}$, $N = 2^{14}$
- $M = 2^{10}$, $N = 2^{20}$
- $M = 2^{20}$, $N = 2^{10}$
