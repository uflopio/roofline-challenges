Compute the log-sum-exp of all elements in a matrix, stably.

Given matrix $A$ of shape $M \times N$, produce the scalar $s$ such that

$$
s = \log\left( \sum_{i=0}^{M-1} \sum_{j=0}^{N-1} \exp\left( A_{i,j} - m \right) \right) + m, \quad m = \max_{i,j} A_{i,j}.
$$

The shift by $m$ is required for numerical stability — without it, $\exp(A_{i,j})$ overflows the float32 range as soon as $A_{i,j}$ exceeds roughly $88$.

## Input

- One matrix $A$ of shape $M \times N$ in row-major order.

## Output

- One scalar $s$.

## Sizes

- $M = 2^{10}$, $N = 2^{10}$
- $M = 2^{12}$, $N = 2^{12}$
- $M = 2^{14}$, $N = 2^{14}$
- $M = 2^{15}$, $N = 2^{15}$
