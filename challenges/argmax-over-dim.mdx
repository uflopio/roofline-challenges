Compute the row-wise argmax of a matrix.

Given matrix $A$ of shape $M \times N$, produce integer vector $\underline{k}$ of length $M$ such that

$$
k_i = \arg\max_{j \in \{0, 1, \dots, N - 1\}} A_{i,j} \quad \text{for } i = 0, 1, \dots, M - 1.
$$

When two entries tie for the maximum, return the smaller index.

## Input

- One matrix $A$ of shape $M \times N$ in row-major order.

## Output

- One integer vector $\underline{k}$ of length $M$.

## Sizes

- $M = 2^{10}$, $N = 2^{10}$
- $M = 2^{12}$, $N = 2^{12}$
- $M = 2^{14}$, $N = 2^{14}$
- $M = 2^{10}$, $N = 2^{20}$
- $M = 2^{20}$, $N = 2^{10}$
