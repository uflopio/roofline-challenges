Compute the element-wise sum of two vectors.

Given vectors $\underline{a}$ and $\underline{b}$ of length $N$, produce vector $\underline{c}$ of length $N$ such that

$$
c_i = a_i + b_i \quad \text{for } i = 0, 1, \dots, N - 1.
$$

## Input

- Two vectors $\underline{a}$ and $\underline{b}$, each of length $N$.

## Output

- One vector $\underline{c}$ of length $N$ such that $c_i = a_i + b_i$.

## Sizes

- $N = 2^{20}$
- $N = 2^{22}$
- $N = 2^{24}$
- $N = 2^{26}$
- $N = 2^{28}$
- $N = 2^{30}$
