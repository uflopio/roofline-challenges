Perform matrix multiplication of a diagonal matrix with another matrix:
$$
C_{i,j} = A[i] \cdot B_{i,j}
$$

## Input
- Diagonal $A$ of size $N$
- Matrix $B$ of size $N \times M$

## Output
- Matrix $C$ of size $N \times M$

## Sizes

- $N = 2^{11}, M = 2^{11}$
- $N = 2^{12}, M = 2^{12}$
- $N = 6144, M = 6144$
- $N = 2^{13}, M = 2^{12}$
